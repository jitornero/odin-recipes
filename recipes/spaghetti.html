Spaghetti

Hoy to do easy spaghetti

Let's try to do it, you will need:

  • spaghetti package
  • water
  • salt
  • cheese
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. To serve: Place a portion of mash in the center of each plate
  3. add cheese to taste
  4. Enjoy!
